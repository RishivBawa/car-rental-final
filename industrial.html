Built for the Job Site

Not Every Road is
Paved

Mining corridors, construction sites, forestry access roads, pipeline right-of-ways — these aren't conditions you put a standard dealer lot truck through. Our industrial fleet solutions are specified, configured, and delivered for the demands of BC's toughest industries.

We work directly with project managers, fleet supervisors, and site leads to spec vehicles that meet worksite safety standards, payload and towing requirements, and the harsh terrain of northern and interior BC.

All vehicles are Latest models, fully covered under manufacturer warranty, with optional Guaranteed Value Program structuring so your project fleet costs stay on budget from mobilization through demobilization. New vehicles mean less downtime and less worry on critical projects where reliability isn't optional.

Configuration Options

Ready for the Job Site

We configure every industrial unit to your specific operational requirements before it arrives on site.

Heavy Payload Packages

Payload upgrades, reinforced beds, and commercial towing packages for demanding hauling requirements.

Worksite Safety Compliance

Configurations aligned with WorkSafeBC requirements for resource sector operations, including lighting and visibility packages.

Extreme Terrain Ready

All-terrain and off-road packages for remote site access — skid plates, diff locks, lifted suspension, all-terrain tires.

Commercial Upfitting

Toolboxes, ladder racks, partition packages, utility shelving, and custom van configurations for trades and service fleets.

Crew Transport Config

Passenger van and large SUV configurations for shuttling crews between camp, staging areas, and remote access points.

Guaranteed Value Program

Industrial fleet on the GVP — fixed cost per vehicle, refresh at agreed intervals, no capital tied up on site.

Fleet Connectivity

OnStar and fleet telematics packages available for real-time tracking, diagnostics, and driver communication on remote projects.

Northern BC Spec

Cold weather packages, block heaters, and winter-spec tires for operations in Prince George, Fort St. John, and the interior.
