Who We Are

Small Team.

FleetAnswers started out of a simple frustration — businesses were overpaying, getting locked into confusing dealer terms, and dealing with accounts nobody at the other end actually knew. So we built something different.

We're a boutique fleet company based in Vancouver, with an office in Prince George serving Northern BC. We carry GMC, Chevrolet, Cadillac, and Ford — brands we trust to perform in BC conditions and hold their value on the road.

Being small is our advantage. Lower overhead means better rates passed directly to you. A focused team means you always talk to someone who knows your account.
