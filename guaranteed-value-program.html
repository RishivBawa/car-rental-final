What is the GVP?

The Guaranteed Value Program is FleetAnswers' flagship offering — a structured, prepaid agreement designed to give your business a fixed cost, the best available price, a new vehicle and the confidence of knowing what your fleet expense will be this year and next.

Think of it as securing high-value savings upfront — combining cost certainty, premium vehicle access, and long-term fleet protection in one tailored program. You stay in a current, fully warranted latest model vehicle, without surprise repair bills and headaches.

Whether you're running a single supervisor truck or managing a 20-vehicle operation, we structure the GVP around your actual usage — your savings goals, your industry and your budget. Every plan is built from scratch, not off a shelf.

Why the GVP Works

Built to Save You Money

Every feature of the Guaranteed Value Program is designed to eliminate cost surprises and give you more control.

01

Secure Best Available Pricing

When you lock in your GVP, you’re securing today’s best available vehicle pricing through a structured prepaid agreement. It’s high-value savings upfront — designed to protect your fleet budget from model-year increases, shifting dealer pricing, and future cost uncertainty.

02

Less Headache, More Roadtime

All GVP vehicles are brand new, latest model vehicles under full manufacturer warranty. The first two oil changes are on us. You’re not inheriting someone else’s maintenance backlog — you’re starting fresh, protected, and ready to work.

03

Periodic Vehicle Refreshes

Upgrade when you want to, not when told. After 6 months of usage you have the option to upgrade, trade in or refresh your fleet with new vehicles. You have the option to upgrade into a newer model year or adjust your vehicle type. Your fleet stays current, your image stays professional, and you’re never stuck with a unit that no longer fits your operation.

04

One Fixed Cost

Your GVP cost is fixed at signing. You know your fleet expense upfront, giving you cleaner budgeting, stronger cost control, and no surprise invoices eating into your margins mid-project.

05

Tailored to Your Operation

Whether it’s 1 truck or 20, every GVP is structured around your actual usage, savings goals, industry, and budget. As your business grows, your plan can grow with it — with a single conversation, not a generic off-the-shelf contract.

06

Dedicated Account Manager

You’ll have one consistent contact at FleetAnswers who knows your fleet, your team, and your operation. No call centres, no repeating yourself, no “let me transfer you.” Just one dedicated person who handles it.

The Process

How It Works

From first conversation to keys in hand — we have answers for every road.

1

Tell Us About Your Operation

Submit an inquiry or give us a call. We'll ask about your industry, fleet size, monthly mileage, and the type of vehicles you need. No pressure, no obligation — just a real conversation.

2

We Build Your Custom Plan

Within 24-72 hours, your account manager structures a GVP around your actual usage. We run the numbers, pick the right models, and put together a plan that fits your budget and kilometres.

3

Review & Lock In Your Rate

We walk you through the plan together — no fine print surprises. Once you're happy, you sign and your rate is locked. That number is now yours for the term, regardless of what happens to market pricing.

4

Keys in Hand

Your vehicle is ready. We deliver it ready to work — oil change included, warranty active, and your account manager is one call away for anything you need.

5

Renew, Refresh, or Adjust

At your renewal interval, we proactively reach out to review your plan. Swap vehicles, adjust fleet size, or carry on — the choice is yours, and we make it easy either way.
